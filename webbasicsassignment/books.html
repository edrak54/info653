I have always loved books. I have a collection of over 100 books in my apartment (the reason I can never move again). In my undergraduate degree, I wrote my thesis about book cover design, to combine my coursework in art history, graphic design, and my interest in books.

*A Note On Bookbinding

For six months in 2018, I was able to work in a university library conservation lab. The lab had two primary elements, book conservation, and material science study of paper. I spent most of my time learning bookbinding as well as assisting with research for a study on mechanical properties of aged paper. My bookbinding work included a variety of different historic and contemporary techniques.
